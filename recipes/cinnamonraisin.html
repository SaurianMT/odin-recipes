Cinnamon Raisin Sourdough

    Ingredients
  • 50g bubbly, active starter
  • 365g warm water
  • 480g bread flour
  • 20g whole wheat flour
  • 9g fine sea salt
    Fillings
  • 65g raisins
  • 65g walnuts
  • 50g sugar
  • 6g cinnamon
    Process
  1. Whisk starter and water together in large bowl with a fork.
  2. Add flour and salt, combine until stiff dough forms.
  3. Cover with damp towel and let rest for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. While dough resting, soak raisins and walnuts in just enough warm water to cover. Drain well before use.
  5. Add raisins and nuts to the bowl.
  6. Gently knead fillings into dough to incorporate, about 1 minute.
  7. Bulk rise - Cover the bowl with damp towel and let rise at room temperature until double in size. About 8-10 hours in 70 degrees.
  8. Dump dough onto lightly floured surface and let rest for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Line a 10-inch oval proofing basket with towel and dust with flour.
  10. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and set aside.
  11. Gently stretch dough into long rectangle, lightly brush surface of dough with water.
  12. Evenly sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mixture over the top, leaving 2-inch border top and bottom, and 1-inch border around sides.
  13. With short end facing you, roll dough into a log, pinching ends to seal. Place in basket, seam side up.
  14. Second rise - Cover dough and let rest 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  15. Preheat oven to 450 degrees and cut parchment paper to fit baking pot.
  16. Place parchment over dough and turn basket to release onto paper.
  17. Make 2-3 diagonal shallow cuts (scores) into surface of dough with small knife.
  18. Transfer into baking pot.
  19. Bake covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered for 40 minutes.
  20. Remove loaf to wire rack and cool for 1 hour before slicing.
