Date Pecan Sourdough

    Ingredients
  • 113g starter
  • 282g warm water
  • 180g all-purpose flour
  • 120g bread flour
  • 60g whole wheat flour
  • 9g fine sea salt
    Fillings
  • 60g toasted pecans - roughly chopped
  • 140g medjool dates - roughly chopped
    Process
  1. Whisk flours and water together in large bowl with a fork.
  2. Let rest 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Fold in starter and salt, combine until stiff dough forms.
  4. Let rest 30 minutes in warm place.
  5. Lamination - Gently stretch dough into long rectangle, lightly brush surface of dough with water.
  6. Evenly sprinkle 3/4 pecans and dates over the top, fold dough up and sprinkle remaining pecans and dates.
  7. With short end facing you, roll dough into a log, pinching ends to seal. Place in basket, seam side up.
  8. Coil foldsPerform 3 sets of coil folds 30 minutes apart.
  9. Cover dough and let rest 1.5 hours.
  10. Place parchment over dough and turn basket to release onto paper.
  11. Cold fermentation - Cover dough and store in refrigerator overnight.
  12. Preheat dutch oven in oven for 30 minutes at 500 degrees
  13. Make 2-3 diagonal shallow cuts (scores) into surface of dough with small knife.
  14. Transfer into baking pot.
  15. Bake covered for 25-30 minutes at 450 degrees, then uncovered for 10-15 minutes.
  16. Remove loaf to wire rack and cool for 1 hour before slicing.
