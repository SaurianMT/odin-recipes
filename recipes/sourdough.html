Basic Sourdough

    Ingredients
  • 50g bubbly, active starter
  • 350g warm water
  • 500g bread flour
  • 9g fine sea salt
    Process
  1. Whisk starter and water together in large bowl with fork.
  2. Add flour and salt, combine until stiff dough forms.
  3. Cover with damp towel and let rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Work dough into smooth ball by folding outside edge into center and repeating all the way around.
  5. Bulk rise - Cover bowl with damp towel and let rise overnight at room temperature.
  6. Dump dough onto lightly floured surface and shape.
  7. Flip dough and let rest for 5-10 minutes
  8. Place dough into bowl seam side up.
  9. Second rise - Cover bowl and let rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  10. Preheat oven to 450 degrees and cut parchment paper to fit baking pot.
  11. Place paper over dough and invert bowl to release dough.
  12. Score surface of dough in cross-hatch pattern with small knife.
  13. Transfer to baking pot.
  14. Bake on center rack for 20 minutes covered, then 30 minutes uncovered.
  15. Remove loaf from baking pot and bake directly on oven rack for 10 minutes to crisp crust.
  16. Transfer to wire rack and cool for 1 hour before slicing.
